zkcreds Application

Issuance Request

As a user, you likely already own some credential (e.g., a driver's license or passport) which not only contains information identifying who you are as the holder of that credential, but also various other permissions regarding what you are and are not allowed to access or otherwise interact with. This information can be arbitrarily encoded as fields, known as attributes. A credential issuer can then systematically analyze these attributes to determine whether you are allowed to have your credential issued. Our system, zkcreds, allows you to request issuance in zero-knowledge, without revealing anything about the attributes you have beyond what is used to satisfy the issuance criteria.

Please provide a JSON-encoded list of attributes for your credential.
